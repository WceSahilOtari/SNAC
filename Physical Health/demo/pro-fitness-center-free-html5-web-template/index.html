Importance of exercise

Why should one exercise ?

The importance of exercise lies in the fact that it’s the best thing which can keep us away from doctors for a very long time. We can’t deny the fact that with the improvement of lifestyle and comfort, problems of health have increased simultaneously. If you want to live a disease free life, the best thing you can do is trying to add exercise to your life. Don’t feel like over-stressing yourself? Exercise can be moderate as well. As a matter of fact, exercises like yoga can be extremely relaxing than anything else.
